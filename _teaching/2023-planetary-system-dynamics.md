This course covers the principles of celestial mechanics, and their application to the Solar System and to extrasolar planetary systems. Specific topics covered include:

1. Planetary system architecture
1. Two and three-body problems
1. Small body forces
1. Close approaches and collisions
1. Disturbing functions
1. Secular and resonant perturbations
